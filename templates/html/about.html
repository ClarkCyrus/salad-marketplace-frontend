ABOUT
SALAD NAVOTEÑOS

SALAD Navoteños is a scout-led fund-raising project in support of Navoteño families that utilizes the lettuce harvested from the GGC-BSP vertical urban farm. The lettuces serve as the main ingredient in the different variations of healthy salads that are sold at an affordable price. The proceeds from the sales of the salads serve as the main source of funding for the project.
